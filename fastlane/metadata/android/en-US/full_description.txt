IdeaSpindle is a creative thinking training app with three modes that make use of your everyday photos.

[Slideshow]
Display photos from your device library with a timer. Filter by album and customize the display interval from 30 seconds to 10 minutes.

[Memory Training]
View a photo for a set time, then try to recall it after it's hidden. Both the viewing duration and blank duration are independently configurable.

[Theme Generator]
Random verb and noun combinations present creative prompts. Flip through them on a timer to expand your thinking. You can add and edit words in the dictionary from the settings screen.

[Key Features]
· Filter photos by album
· Settings saved between sessions
· Japanese / English display
· Customizable word dictionary
· Tablet and landscape support

No ads, no purchases, no internet connection required.
